## about me

- Founder of **Study Share HQ**, an AI-driven SAT, ACT, and PSAT study platform used by **4,000+ teens** across **75+ districts**.
- Founder and solo developer of **Seat Maker**, a Swift seating-chart app that reached **#4 in U.S. Paid Lifestyle** within 24 hours.
- Co-founder and leader in **Homework Helpers**, a PTA-sponsored peer tutoring program serving **35% of the district**.
- Founder and lead instructor of **CodeLab**, where students built **95 mobile apps** across coding sessions.
- Interested in product, SwiftUI, growth, design, automation, and building things people actually use.
